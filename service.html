Dosage, side effects, and withdrawals of common antidepressants

This page covers the dosage, side effects, and withdrawal symptoms of the representative drugs in the four main classes of antidepressants: SSRI, SNRI, TCA, and MAOI. Each drug class has distinct characteristics, so providing information on the representative drugs can offer important insights for patients when choosing a medication.

Fluoxetine - Prozac

Dosage: Begin with 20 mg once daily for depression. If needed, increase to 20 mg twice daily (morning and noon), up to a maximum of 80 mg per day.

Side Effects: Possible nausea, headache, insomnia, diarrhea, and fatigue.

Withdrawal Symptoms: May include dizziness, sleep disturbances, and anxiety.

Paroxetine - Serotax

Dosage: Start with 12.5 mg once daily, increasing weekly by 12.5 mg, with a max daily dose of 60 mg.

Side Effects: May cause appetite loss, blurred vision, dry mouth, constipation, diarrhea, dizziness, tremors, headaches, insomnia, and sexual dysfunction.

Withdrawal Symptoms: Include dizziness, sensory disturbances, restlessness, confusion, palpitations, and headache.

Fluvoxamine - Dumirox

Dosage: Typically 100-200 mg divided into 2-3 daily doses, potentially increased to 300 mg.

Side Effects: Generally, no serious side effects reported.

Withdrawal Symptoms: Possible dizziness, sleep disturbances, anxiety, and headaches.

Sertraline - Zoloft

Dosage: Maintain at 50-100 mg daily, adjusting by 50 mg increments, up to a maximum of 200 mg daily.

Side Effects: Can cause drowsiness, sexual dysfunction, and gastrointestinal issues.

Withdrawal Symptoms: May involve dizziness, sleep disturbances, and anxiety.

Escitalopram - Lexapro

Dosage: Typically 10 mg once daily, can increase to 20 mg based on patient response.

Side Effects: Possible anxiety, agitation, abnormal dreams, and decreased libido.

Withdrawal Symptoms: Might include dizziness, sleep disturbances, and anxiety.

Vortioxetine - Brintellix

Dosage: Initiate with 10 mg once daily, with a range of 5-20 mg based on patient response.

Side Effects: Commonly nausea, headache, vomiting, dizziness, and abdominal pain.

Withdrawal Symptoms: Information not specified.

Amitriptyline - Etravil, Enafon

Dosage: Generally 30-75 mg daily, possibly increased to 150-300 mg.

Side Effects: Commonly dry mouth, orthostatic hypotension.

Withdrawal Symptoms: Information not specified.

Nortriptyline - Sensival

Dosage: Typically 10-25 mg three times daily, up to 150 mg daily.

Side Effects: Commonly dry mouth, difficulty urinating, blurred vision, constipation, and dizziness.

Withdrawal Symptoms: Might include dizziness, sleep disturbances, and anxiety.

Clomipramine - Gromin

Dosage: Start at 10 mg daily, gradually increased to 30-150 mg for maintenance.

Side Effects: Potential nausea, dry mouth, fatigue, and dizziness.

Withdrawal Symptoms: May involve dizziness, sleep disturbances, and anxiety.

Imipramine - Imipramine hydrochloride

Dosage: Typically 25-75 mg daily, gradually increased if needed.

Side Effects: Potential orthostatic hypotension, ECG abnormalities.

Withdrawal Symptoms: Information not specified.

Amoxapine - Adisen

Dosage: Generally 25-150 mg once daily or divided into 2-3 doses.

Side Effects: Commonly drowsiness, dry mouth, constipation, and blurred vision.

Withdrawal Symptoms: Information not specified.

Duloxetine - Cymbalta

Dosage: Typically 60 mg once daily, with no additional benefit beyond 60 mg.

Side Effects: May cause numbness, fatigue, loss of libido, and decreased exercise ability.

Withdrawal Symptoms: Include dizziness, sensory disturbances, fatigue, and insomnia.

Venlafaxine - Effexor

Dosage: Usually 75 mg once daily, starting at 37.5 mg for adaptation.

Side Effects: Possible nausea, irritable bowel syndrome, dizziness, and increased blood pressure.

Withdrawal Symptoms: Include reduced appetite, anxiety, confusion, and sensory disturbances.

Desvenlafaxine - Pristiq

Dosage: Typically 50 mg once daily.

Side Effects: May cause nausea, dizziness, insomnia, and constipation.

Withdrawal Symptoms: Similar to venlafaxine.

Milnacipran - Ixel

Dosage: Initiate with 25 mg daily, gradually increase to 50 mg twice daily.

Side Effects: Potential constipation, facial flushing, and dysesthesia.

Withdrawal Symptoms: Information not specified.

Moclobemide - Aurorix

Dosage: Start with 300 mg daily, divided into 2-3 doses after meals.

Side Effects: Possible nervousness, insomnia, and occasional confusion.

Withdrawal Symptoms: May involve nervousness, insomnia, and confusion. Suicidal thoughts reported.

Bupropion - Wellbutrin

Dosage: Begin with 150 mg once daily, then increase to 150 mg twice daily.

Side Effects: Possible insomnia, unusual dreams, anxiety, and tremors.

Withdrawal Symptoms: Information not specified.

Mirtazapine - Remeron

Dosage: Start with 15 mg once daily, increase as needed up to 45 mg daily.

Side Effects: Commonly causes drowsiness, increased appetite, and weight gain.

Withdrawal Symptoms: May involve dizziness, excitement, anxiety, and restlessness.

Trazodone - Trittico

Dosage: Initiate with 12.5 mg three times daily, limit to twice daily for specific groups.

Side Effects: Potential muscle pain, blurred vision, and difficulty urinating.

Withdrawal Symptoms: Information not specified.

Tianeptine - Stabron

Dosage: Administer 12.5 mg three times daily orally before meals.

Side Effects: Potential muscle pain, blurred vision, and gastrointestinal discomfort.

Withdrawal Symptoms: Information not specified.
